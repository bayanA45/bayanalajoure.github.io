UAV / Defense · 2024–2026
Albayraq — Jordan's First Fully Composite Surveillance UAV
What it is

Albayraq is a pioneering student-led research project to design and manufacture Jordan's first fully composite surveillance UAV. The project united mechanical, electrical, and AI expertise over two years — from research and theory through design, testing, and prototyping — culminating in a working 3.2m fixed-wing UAV.

My role — Electrical Systems Engineer

PCB Design: Designed a custom 2-layer, multi-rail Power Distribution Board (PDB) — 4–6S battery input, four regulated rails (3.3V, 5V, 7.4V, 12V), 24A max continuous, safety and protection circuits. High-power loads (Jetson Nano) powered directly from battery for electrical isolation.

Curing oven: Built the complete electrical system for an industrial composite-curing oven reaching 100°C — 220V wiring, relay control, BMO180 temperature sensor, indicator lighting.

Systems integration: Wiring harness design, component procurement, cross-department coordination, full financial tracking and documentation.

Capstone · Robotics · 2026
Bunyan — Modular Self-Assembling Robot
🏆 1st Place — HTU Capstone Expo 2026
Concept

Inspired by MIT's M-Blocks and Big Hero 6, Bunyan is a modular robotics system consisting of a builder robot and passive voxel cells. The builder autonomously picks up voxels and places them with precision to construct structures — with the long-term vision of robots assembling other robots.

My contributions

Mathematical model: Developed a fully verified kinematic and dynamic model for one robot limb — tested and validated in Autodesk Inventor.

Digital twin: Built from scratch a live digital twin integrating MATLAB App Designer and Autodesk Inventor. Users control parameters via MATLAB sliders with live updates reflected instantly in Inventor.

Mechanical design: Co-designed the limb structure and a novel magnetic joint system with no prior design precedent.

Prototyping: Fabricated components using SLA resin, PETG, and PLA 3D printing.

Unmanned Ground Vehicle — IMU Navigation & Sensor Fusion
Project

Personal project with a collaborator: build a robot that autonomously navigates from point A to B using GPS for position and IMU for spatial orientation — no human control. Reached a fully functional prototype before the project was paused to focus on Albayraq.

CNC 4th-Axis Mod & Lab Hardware Engineering
CNC Router — 4th Axis Engineering

The OMNI CNC router was a 3-axis machine. Added a 4th axis driver and relay switch system that toggles between the Y and U axes — giving the machine 4-axis capability without adding a 4th motor. Rewired all on/off switches, added an emergency stop button, and rebuilt the full electrical connection. The modification works and is in active use.

3D Printer Troubleshooting

Diagnosed and repaired 3 non-functional lab printers (Prusa, Ultimaker, Ender series) through systematic troubleshooting. All returned to service for 80+ daily users. Also attempted a 4th-axis motor and driver addition on the laser cutter.
